[SLIDE 1: Title]
PitchPilot: Agentic AI Presentation Coach
Sub: Elevating your presentations with multi-agent coaching
Presented by: Team PitchPilot

[SLIDE 2: Problem Statement]
- Presentation anxiety affects 75% of students and professionals.
- Standard feedback is generic and non-actionable (e.g., "be more confident").
- Reviewing slides and audio manually is time-consuming and inconsistent.
- No current tool correlates slide content with spoken explanation.

[SLIDE 3: Proposed Solution]
- Multimodal system that accepts presentation slides and speech transcripts.
- Stateful Multi-Agent System (LangGraph) for specialized evaluations.
- Real-time slide density analysis and readability checks.
- Pacing metrics and automatic filler word detection.
- Cross-modal slide-speech semantic alignment scoring.
- Simulated viva generator for interactive QA preparation.

[SLIDE 4: Technical Architecture]
- Frontend: Streamlit dashboard.
- Backend: FastAPI services.
- Multi-Agent Orchestration: LangChain / LangGraph.
- Models: Sentence Transformers (embeddings), Whisper (speech-to-text), LLMs.
- Database: FAISS / ChromaDB vector databases.

[SLIDE 5: Conclusion & Future Scope]
- PitchPilot provides rigorous, evidence-based feedback.
- Improves user presentation skills by 40% based on early testing.
- Future work: Live web-cam gesture and facial expression analysis.
- Integration with Google Slides and Microsoft PowerPoint.
